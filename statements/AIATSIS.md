# AIATSIS Artificial Intelligence Transparency Statement

This artificial intelligence (AI) transparency statement provides the public with relevant information about AIATSIS use of AI. This statement is required under the Policy for the responsible use of AI in government (the Policy).

## Why do we use AI

The AIATSIS vision is a world in which Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander knowledge and cultures are recognised, respected, celebrated and valued. We use AI where it can support the achievement of our vision and our strategic priority areas as outlined in our corporate plan. We are committed to continually improving our capabilities to meet current and future needs, and AI is becoming increasingly important to ensure we foster innovation and adaptiveness in how we tell the story of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Australia, create opportunities for people to encounter, engage with and be transformed by that story, and support and facilitate Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultural resurgence and shape our national narrative.

We engage AI-assisted tools where they will support us to improve the efficiency and accessibility of our legislated functions and deliver innovative digital and data-driven client services and staff experiences.

## How do we govern AI

The AIATSIS Chief AI Officer is the Executive Director of the Ngurra Transformation Group.

The AIATSIS Accountable Official for AI is the Chief Information Officer. The Accountable Official is accountable for governance of AI and implementation of the policy within AIATSIS.

To ensure appropriate monitoring and governance of AI, AIATSIS has implemented the Policy for the responsible use of AI in government, the Technical standard for government’s use of artificial intelligence and application of Australia’s AI Ethics Principles. AIATSIS has also implemented additional risk-scaled governance mechanisms for example, an internal AI Usage Register.

## What types of AI do we use?

AIATSIS currently uses AI in the following domains and usage patterns. More information about AI usage patterns and domains is available on the DTA website.

### Domains

Corporate and enabling: Supports corporate functions, including HR, finance, media and communications, and IT, by automating processes, optimising resource allocation and improving operational efficiency.

### Usage Patterns

Workplace productivity: Automates routine tasks, manage workflows, and facilitate communication.

## How do we comply with the Policy?

AIATSIS complies with all mandatory requirements of the Policy. This includes listing our Chief AI Officer and Accountable Official for AI with the Digital Transformation Agency, ensuring this transparency statement is available on our website, and reviewing this statement annually or sooner, as outlined below. AIATSIS has also implemented or is working towards all discretionary recommendations.

## When will this transparency statement be updated?

This statement will be updated at least once a year, or sooner if we make any significant change to our approach to AI, or if any new factor materially impacts the existing statement’s accuracy.

## Who can I talk to?

For any enquires regarding AIATSIS’ use of AI please use our Corporate and Governance Contact Form.
